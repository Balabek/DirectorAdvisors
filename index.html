What We Do

Director Advisors is a full-service funeral recruitment agency that has successfully connected funeral directors with funeral home owners. Our team of recruiters is excited about the prospect of discovering openings that work with you. We are a team of professionals ready to provide the necessary arrangements to connect funeral directors and funeral home owners based on several criteria.

Whether you are a funeral home owner or a funeral director looking for an employment opportunity, our team is dedicated to use all our available resources at hand to make it possible to provide the service you are looking for. Our team is constantly reviewing applications to pick the most qualified funeral directors to connect funeral home owners with the most qualified funeral director. With our broad approach to funeral career placements, we aim to create a win-win situation for both the hiring funeral home and the funeral director.

If you are an experienced funeral director professional and would like to be kept in mind for job opportunities that come up, send us your resume and complete the online profile. By doing so we can consider your qualifications for funeral director positions that come up in the future. We have helped numerous other funeral professionals land these great job opportunities.
