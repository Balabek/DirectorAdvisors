About Us

Who We Are

Director Advisors is an experienced team of funeral industry professionals with decades of experience based in St. Petersburg, Florida with the mission to assist funeral home owners in their search to find funeral directors when they have an opening. We also build long-term partnerships with employers to resolve the hiring challenges by providing the best candidates in the marketplace.

We are creating a window of opportunities for both the employer and employee connecting qualified individuals all over the United Sates. By taking advantage of our services, you will receive quality service that fits your needs. None can help you achieve this faster than Director Advisors.
